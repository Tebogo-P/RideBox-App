Our Story

Who We Are & What We Stand For

RideBox was born from a simple frustration: South Africa's e-hailing landscape left too many people behind — vulnerable to load shedding, unsafe rides, and unpredictable pricing. Two CPUT graduates from Soweto and Port Elizabeth decided to change that. Built on solar power and a commitment to community safety, RideBox is rewriting the rules of urban mobility by bridging the "Last-Mile Communication Gap".

Mission
To deliver safe, affordable, and energy-independent e-hailing access to every South African — ensuring that a lack of smartphone access, data, or power never leaves you stranded.
Vision
A South Africa where clean, connected mobility is a right — not a privilege — powered entirely by renewable energy.

Core Values

What We Offer

The RideBox Product & Platform

01
Solar-Powered Booking Hubs
Physical RideBox units deployed at airports, malls, universities, and public transport nodes. Each unit runs entirely on embedded solar arrays and lithium battery backups, fully operational for 8-12 hours during any stage of load shedding.
02
Secure E-Hailing Platform
A vetted driver network featuring facial verification, an SOS panic button, and support for all 11 official languages via an AI-assisted UI. No surge pricing, no surprises — just safe, reliable transport.
03
Tourist & Traveller Protection
Dedicated lanes and pre-screened drivers at major airports protect visitors from scams and unauthorized drivers. Our AI handles currency conversions and compares prices across all platforms to ensure fair, market-rate rides.
04
Driver Partner Programme
Join the RideBox driver network with transparent earning structures, training support, and access to our growing national route network across 21 pilot locations.
05
Municipal & Enterprise Partnerships
We work with municipalities, universities, and corporate campuses to integrate RideBox hubs into existing transport infrastructure at zero grid cost.
06
Investor & Franchise Opportunities
Own a RideBox unit in your city. Our franchise model allows local operators to deploy and manage hubs, with passive Ad Revenue generated from sunlight-readable digital screens on Side B of each kiosk.

FAQ

Common Questions, Answered Directly

RideBox is a solar-powered e-hailing platform built for South African conditions. Unlike app-only competitors, RideBox operates physical booking hubs that work without internet or electricity — meaning you can book a safe, regulated ride even during Stage 6 load shedding. We also use fixed fares with no surge pricing.

Every RideBox unit is equipped with embedded solar arrays and lithium battery storage. Our hubs operate at 0% grid dependency — they charge during daylight and maintain full operational capacity through any stage of Eskom load shedding. No wifi? No problem. Our units use low-bandwidth cellular as a fallback.

We are currently in a 21-unit national pilot phase, targeting 3 units per major province at high-traffic locations including major airports, shopping malls, and universities. Contact us for a full list of current and upcoming locations or to request a hub in your area.

All RideBox driver partners undergo a multi-step vetting process including criminal background checks, vehicle roadworthiness inspection, and a platform onboarding assessment. Drivers are continuously rated by passengers, and our operations team conducts regular compliance reviews. Every booking also utilizes facial verification to ensure the person booking is protected.

RideBox operates on a fixed-fare model. The price you see before you confirm your booking is the price you pay — no surge pricing, no hidden fees, no last-minute changes. For tourists, our AI handles currency conversions and compares prices across all platforms to ensure they get a fair, market-rate ride.

We work with municipalities, universities, airports, and commercial property owners to deploy RideBox hubs within existing transport infrastructure. Partnerships are flexible — from sponsored hubs to full franchise agreements where operators can earn from the ad screens. Reach out via our contact form to request a proposal.

National Deployment

Our initial 21-unit pilot program targets 3 units per major province at critical high-traffic hubs to solve accessibility gaps:

  • 1 Unit at the Province's Largest Airport: Targeted at tourists vulnerable to being overcharged. Our AI handles currency conversions and compares prices to ensure fair, market-rate rides.
  • 1 Unit at the Province's Largest University: Serving the youth demographic running out of data or battery.
  • 1 Unit at the Province's Largest Shopping Mall: Capturing high foot-traffic for users needing a reliable way home.

0% Grid Dependency

Engineered to remain 100% operational during load shedding and infrastructure failure.

  • Power Reliability: Each unit is equipped with solar panels and lithium battery backups, providing independent runtime.
  • Vandal-Proof: Constructed from powder-coated steel or aluminum with 6-8mm tempered glass and a 4-bolt concrete anchor system.

Secure Access

Vetted driver platforms and integrated emergency response features for peace of mind.

  • Every unit features facial verification to ensure the person booking the ride is protected.
  • An integrated SOS panic button links directly to safety partners.
