Our Story

Who We Are & What We Stand For

RideBox was born from a simple frustration: South Africa's e-hailing landscape left too many people vulnerable to load shedding, unsafe rides, and unpredictable pricing. Two CPUT graduates from Soweto and Port Elizabeth decided to change that. Built on solar power and a commitment to community safety, RideBox is rewriting the rules of urban mobility by bridging the last-mile communication gap.

Mission
To deliver safe, affordable, and energy-independent e-hailing access to every South African, ensuring that a lack of smartphone access, data, or power never leaves you stranded.
Vision
A South Africa where clean, connected mobility is a right not a privilege powered entirely by renewable energy.

What We Offer

FAQ

Common Questions, Answered Directly

RideBox is a solar-powered e-hailing platform built for South African conditions. Unlike app-only competitors, RideBox operates physical booking hubs that work without internet or electricity — meaning you can book a safe, regulated ride even during heavy load shedding. We also use fixed fares with absolutely no surge pricing.

Every RideBox unit is equipped with embedded solar arrays and lithium battery storage. Our hubs operate entirely independent of the grid, meaning they charge during daylight and maintain full operational capacity through any power failure. If the local Wi-Fi goes down, our units use a low-bandwidth cellular fallback to keep you connected.

We are currently operating a 21-unit national pilot phase, targeting key high-traffic locations including major airports, shopping malls, and university campuses. Please reach out to our team for a complete, up-to-date list of current and upcoming locations or to request a hub in your specific area.

All RideBox driver partners undergo a comprehensive vetting process that includes strict criminal background checks, vehicle roadworthiness inspections, and detailed platform onboarding assessments. Drivers are continuously rated by passengers, and our operations team conducts regular compliance reviews. Every booking also utilizes facial verification to ensure maximum protection.

RideBox operates strictly on a fixed-fare model. The price you see before you confirm your booking is the exact price you will pay. There are no surge multipliers, no hidden fees, and no last-minute changes. For international tourists, our system automatically handles currency conversions and compares regional prices to guarantee a fair market rate.

We partner heavily with municipalities, universities, airports, and commercial property owners to seamlessly deploy RideBox hubs within their existing transport infrastructure. Our partnerships are highly flexible, ranging from sponsored corporate hubs to full franchise agreements where operators can earn additional income from the integrated ad screens.

National Deployment

Our initial 21-unit pilot program strategically targets key locations with three units per major province, specifically positioned at high-traffic hubs to solve critical accessibility gaps. We install units at the largest provincial airports to protect tourists from being overcharged, utilizing our automated systems to handle currency conversions and ensure fair, market-rate rides.

Additionally, we target major university campuses to support students who may run out of data or battery, as well as large shopping malls to provide a safe and reliable way home for high volumes of daily foot traffic.

0% Grid Dependency

The RideBox platform is uniquely engineered to remain completely operational during severe load shedding and unexpected infrastructure failures. Each unit functions independently thanks to its built-in solar panels and robust lithium battery backups, ensuring a reliable power source regardless of the grid status.

To guarantee longevity and passenger safety, the kiosks are designed to be entirely vandal-proof. They are constructed from highly durable powder-coated steel or aluminum, feature 6-8mm tempered glass, and are secured to the ground using a heavy-duty four-bolt concrete anchor system.

Secure Access

We prioritize secure access and ultimate peace of mind by partnering exclusively with fully vetted driver platforms and integrating essential emergency response features into every kiosk.

Every single unit utilizes advanced facial verification technology to guarantee that the person booking the ride is fully protected. Furthermore, an integrated SOS panic button is readily accessible, providing a direct and immediate link to our dedicated safety and security partners.
